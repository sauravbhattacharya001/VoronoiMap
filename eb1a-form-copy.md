# EB-1A Readiness Check — Google Form Copy

## Form Title
EB-1A Readiness Check

## Form Description
Thinking about an EB-1A green card? Fill out this quick form and I'll send you a free initial read on where you stand — which criteria your profile might map to and what gaps to focus on.

Takes ~3 minutes. No spam, no sales pitch — just a straight assessment.

---

## Questions

### 1. Full Name
Type: Short text
Required: Yes

### 2. Email
Type: Short text
Required: Yes

### 3. Current Role & Company
Type: Short text
Required: Yes
Placeholder: e.g., Senior Software Engineer at Google

### 4. Years of Experience
Type: Dropdown
Options:
- 0-3 years
- 4-7 years
- 8-12 years
- 13+ years

### 5. What field would you claim extraordinary ability in?
Type: Short text
Required: Yes
Helper text: Be specific — e.g., "distributed systems," "machine learning for healthcare," "cybersecurity incident response" — not just "computer science"

### 6. Which of these do you have? (check all that apply)
Type: Checkboxes
Options:
- Published papers in peer-reviewed journals or conferences
- Awards or prizes for excellence in my field
- Media coverage about me or my work (articles, interviews, features)
- Membership in associations that require outstanding achievement
- Evidence of high salary relative to others in my field
- Patents or original technical contributions with real-world impact
- Experience judging or reviewing others' work (peer review, panels, competitions)
- Led or played a critical role at a well-known organization
- Authored contributions to my field (books, widely-used tools, frameworks)
- None of the above yet

### 7. Briefly describe your strongest achievement
Type: Long text
Required: Yes
Helper text: The one thing you're most proud of professionally. Could be a project you led, a paper you published, a system you built, an award you received. 2-3 sentences is fine.

### 8. Where are you in the process?
Type: Multiple choice
Options:
- Just exploring — not sure if I qualify
- Researching actively — reading about EB-1A criteria
- Working with an attorney but want a second opinion
- Filed already and got an RFE (Request for Evidence)
- Previously denied — considering refiling

### 9. What's your biggest question or concern about EB-1A?
Type: Long text
Required: No
Helper text: Optional but helpful — this lets me give you a more useful response

### 10. How did you find me?
Type: Multiple choice
Options:
- LinkedIn
- Topmate
- Referral from a friend/colleague
- Google search
- Other
