# Life Goals

## 🧘 Consciousness & Presence
- **Become Ramana-like — abide in the Self permanently.** Not as philosophy but as lived reality. Constant awareness of "I AM" — the observer behind thought. Self-inquiry as natural as breathing. No effort, just being.
- **The method: Atma Vichara (Self-inquiry).** Trace the "I" back to its source. Who is the one aware? Follow that feeling of "I" inward — not the thought "I", but the sense of being itself. Every thought, every sensation arises *to* someone. Find that someone. That's the entire practice.
